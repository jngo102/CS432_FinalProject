Instructions

This is a relatively simple simulation of our solar system. It includes the Sun and the nine planets within it. Interplanetary distances are not accurate, but planet rotations and orbit speeds are scaled according to real data.

The simulation will take some time before it begins to run smoothly as it loads up models and textures.

If you see a gray background instead of a space-themed skybox, please perform a regular refresh of your page (F5).

Click on the right half of the canvas to capture your mouse cursor and prevent it from locking at the edges of the screen. Press 'escape' to show your cursor again.

Controls

These are the flight controls for your perspective in the simulation.
Key/Mouse Action
W Fly forward
S Fly backward
A Fly left
D Fly right
Space Fly up
Shift Fly down
Mouse move Look around
You can also press a key in the number row to get a more detailed view of a celestial body. Press the same key again to regain control.
Key Body
0 Sun
1 Mercury
2 Venus
3 Earth
4 Mars
5 Jupiter
6 Saturn
7 Uranus
8 Neptune
